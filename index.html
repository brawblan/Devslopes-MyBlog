Types of Drums Made By Pearl

Drum Sets

Pearl has been making drums since 1946. They were founded in Japan and specialized in percussion instruments. Pearl's Drum Sets can be seen all over the world on stages and in bars alike. These drum kits are dependable and look great, too!

Marching Drums

Pearl has been in the Marching Percussion market since the '70s and has led the way in innovation and style. The introduction of the high tension snare drum revolutionized the marching world forever.

World Percussion

Pearl is a go-to when it comes to World Percussion instruments. They have been making high quality world percussion instruments for many years. They continue to develop new instruments and help bring the great music of the world to new places.
